For employee and workforce matters, Level 2 assessment examines if companies provide KPIs on key issues corresponding to core labour rights. Individual criteria reflect international standards and the biggest current challenges. For human rights matters, Level 2 assesses the comprehensiveness of companies' disclosure based on the criteria derived from the UN Guiding Principles on Business and Human Rights Reporting Framework.
